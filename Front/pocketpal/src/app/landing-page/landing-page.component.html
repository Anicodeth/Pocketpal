PocketPal

PocketPal is the ultimate web app for university students to track their expenses and achieve their financial goals. With our budgeting tool, financial education resources, user-friendly interface, and personal financial assistant AI, PocketPal is designed to make managing finances easy and stress-free. Sign up now to start taking control of your finances with PocketPal.

Financial education resources

Access to financial education resources to help students improve their financial literacy

User-friendly interface

A user-friendly interface that will be easy for students to navigate and use
